He has delivered many corporate trainings on .NET, .NET Core technologies, Microsoft SQL Server, Cloud Computing, Big Data - Hadoop and AI & ML.

He spends a lot of time in understanding the frameworks and explores the internal workings. Since he knows most of the internals, he delivers training with confidence.

While delivering trainings, he never uses high level jargons and presentations. He goes to low level and makes the participants visualize everything. Once you attend his training, you will be able to explain any concepts clearly and will be able to answer any questions logically in interviews. And with the same knowledge you will be able to work on real-world projects
